Frequently asked questions

Is this therapy? +

No. Unsnag is a self-guided emotional processing tool, not a clinical intervention. It's designed for the everyday moments when a pattern activates and you want to move through it — not a replacement for professional mental health support.

What if I don't have much time? +

The UNSNAG process is designed to be done in minutes. The 90-second Notice step is the only timed part — everything else moves at your pace.

Do I need to know anything about somatic work? +

Not at all. The app guides you through every step. Just show up with whatever's going on.

What does 'moving' an emotion actually mean? +

When emotion isn't processed, it stays stuck in the body as sensation — tension, tightness, a racing heart. Giving it space to be felt allows it to complete its cycle and dissipate. You'll feel the difference.

Is my data private and secure? +

Yes. Your account is password protected and your sessions belong to you alone. What you share inside Unsnag stays inside Unsnag.

What happens after my free trial? +

After 14 days, your subscription is $5/month for unlimited use. You can cancel any time.

Will this actually change anything, or just help me feel better in the moment? +

Both — but in that order. Each session processes what's happening right now so you're not carrying it. Over time, you're also building a new default response. The pattern that made sense at some point starts to lose its grip. You'll notice it first in small moments: you don't spiral over the text. You say the thing. You let someone be disappointed without it meaning something about you.
