Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Who We Are

Unsnag is an emotional processing app created and operated by Afi Catena. We're a small, independent product — not a giant company. When you share something with us, we take that seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what we do (and don't do) with it.

If you have questions, you can reach us directly at afi@unsnag.co.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the service and make it better:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up for a free trial or paid subscription.
  • Lead information — your email address when you use the free Yours or Not tool. This is stored in our database to send you follow-up emails about Unsnag. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • Usage data — which features you use, how often you open the app, and how you move through sessions. We use Google Analytics (GA4) on the marketing website and Vercel Analytics on the app for aggregate usage data.
  • Session content — the text or voice input you enter during a guided session or the Yours or Not tool, stored securely and used only to deliver the service to you. We do not use your personal responses to train AI models or for advertising.
  • Device and technical data — browser type, device type, and general location (country/region) to help us debug issues and improve performance.
  • Payment information — processed through Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

How We Use It

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Unsnag app experience
  • Send you product updates, nurture emails, and occasional emails related to your use of Unsnag (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Diagnose technical problems and understand how features are being used
  • Process your subscription payments through Stripe
  • Comply with legal obligations if required

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your session content for advertising or to train AI models used by third parties.

Third Parties We Work With

We use a small set of trusted services to operate Unsnag:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and server functions. Your account data and session content are stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure (hosted on AWS). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI processing for session validation and the Yours or Not tool. Your session text is sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We never see or store your full card number. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Resend — transactional and nurture emails. We share only your email address and first name.
  • Vercel — hosting for the Unsnag app. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy.
  • Netlify — hosting for the marketing website (unsnag.co).
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — aggregate usage analytics on the marketing website. GA4 collects anonymized browsing data. We do not enable advertising features or share data with Google for ad targeting.
  • Vercel Analytics — privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics on the app. No cookies, no personal data collection.

We do not share your personal information with data brokers, advertisers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

Your Session Data

What you write or say during a session is yours. Session content is stored in your account so you can access your history. We do not read, review, or analyze your session content for any purpose other than delivering the service to you.

When you use the free Yours or Not tool, the situation you describe is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your results. This text is not stored by Anthropic and is not used to train AI models.

You can delete your session history at any time from your account settings. When you delete it, it's gone — we don't retain it in backups beyond a standard 30-day window.

Data Retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel and delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal or financial compliance (e.g., payment records).

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR for EU residents and state privacy laws like the CCPA for California residents). These may include the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell your data, but you have the right to ask)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at afi@unsnag.co. We'll respond within 30 days.

Unsnag is operated from New Jersey, United States. By using the service, your data may be processed in the United States.

Cookies

The Unsnag app uses essential cookies (via Supabase auth) to keep you logged in and maintain session state. The marketing website uses Google Analytics cookies for aggregate traffic analysis. We don't use advertising cookies or retargeting pixels. If you block all cookies, some features may not work correctly.

Children's Privacy

Unsnag is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll let you know by email or with a notice in the app before the changes take effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last updated.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or just want to know more? Email us at afi@unsnag.co. It's just me and I read everything.
