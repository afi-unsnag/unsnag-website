Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2025

Who We Are

Unsnag is an emotional processing app created and operated by Afi Catena. We're a small, independent product — not a giant company. When you share something with us, we take that seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what we do (and don't do) with it.

If you have questions, you can reach us directly at hello@unsnag.co.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the service and make it better:

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up or join the waitlist.
  • Usage data — which features you use, how often you open the app, and how you move through sessions. This is anonymous aggregate data, not tied to your specific responses.
  • Session content — the text or voice input you enter during a guided session, stored securely and used only to complete that session. We do not analyze your personal responses to train AI models or for advertising.
  • Device and technical data — browser type, device type, and general location (country/region) to help us debug issues and improve performance.
  • Payment information — processed through Stripe. We never see or store your full card number. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

How We Use It

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Unsnag app experience
  • Send you product updates, early access notifications, and occasional emails you signed up for (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • Diagnose technical problems and understand how features are being used
  • Process your subscription payments through Stripe
  • Comply with legal obligations if required

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your session content for advertising or to train AI models used by third parties.

Third Parties We Work With

We use a small set of trusted services to operate Unsnag:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's own privacy policy.
  • Email provider (e.g., Resend or similar) — for transactional emails and product updates. We share only your email address.
  • Hosting and infrastructure — cloud providers used to host the app. Data is stored in encrypted form.
  • Analytics — privacy-respecting, aggregate-only analytics to understand usage patterns. We do not use Google Analytics or any platform that builds advertising profiles from your data.

We do not share your personal information with data brokers, advertisers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

Your Session Data

What you write or say during a session is yours. We store it only long enough to complete the session and, if you choose, to provide continuity between sessions (so you don't lose your place). We do not read your session content for any purpose other than delivering the service to you.

You can delete your session history at any time from your account settings. When you delete it, it's gone — we don't retain it in backups beyond a standard 30-day window.

Data Retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. If you cancel and delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal or financial compliance (e.g., payment records).

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@unsnag.co. We'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use a small number of essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain session state. We don't use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. If you block all cookies, some features may not work correctly.

Children's Privacy

Unsnag is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll let you know by email or with a notice in the app before the changes take effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects when it was last updated.

Contact

Questions, concerns, or just want to know more? Email us at hello@unsnag.co. We're a small team and we read everything.
