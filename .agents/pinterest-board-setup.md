# Pinterest Board Setup Guide

## Account Setup

- **Account type:** Business (free — gives analytics + rich pins)
- **Display name:** Unsnag — Emotional Wellness for Overthinkers
- **Username:** @unsnag
- **Website:** unsnag.co
- **Profile photo:** Afi headshot or the "u" logo
- **Bio:** A guided app that helps you feel what's stuck instead of just thinking about it. 6 steps. A few minutes. Built for women who overthink, people-please, and carry everyone else's stuff. Created by Afi Catena.

## Boards to Create

### 1. Stop Overthinking
**Description:** Tips and insights for women who can't get out of their own heads. How to stop spiraling and start choosing differently.
**What goes here:** Blog pins from Feelings pillar, quote cards about overthinking

### 2. People-Pleasing Recovery
**Description:** For the woman who's tired of saying yes when she means no. Boundaries, accountability, and letting go of what's not yours.
**What goes here:** Blog pins from Patterns pillar, quote cards about people-pleasing

### 3. Feel Your Feelings
**Description:** What it actually means to feel a feeling instead of just thinking about it. Somatic processing, emotional wellness, body-based tools.
**What goes here:** Blog pins from Feelings pillar, educational content

### 4. Being Yourself
**Description:** Permission to stop performing. Identity, self-worth, and what happens when you stop needing everyone's approval.
**What goes here:** Blog pins from Identity pillar, quote cards

### 5. Unsnag App
**Description:** A guided app for processing emotions in the moment. See how it works, what users are saying, and why it's different.
**What goes here:** Product pins, testimonial pins, "how it works" pins

### 6. Parenting & Feelings
**Description:** How you handle your feelings teaches your kid how to handle theirs. Emotional wellness for moms who want to break the cycle.
**What goes here:** Parenting blog pins, quote cards

### 7. Blog
**Description:** All articles from the Unsnag blog — feelings, patterns, identity, and what it actually takes to stop overthinking.
**What goes here:** Every blog post pin goes here + its topical board

---

## Pin Rules

- Pin every blog post to TWO boards: the topic board + the Blog board
- Pin format: 1000 x 1500px (tall)
- Include "unsnag.co/blog" on every pin
- Use keywords in pin titles and descriptions (same as blog SEO keywords)

---

## Enable Rich Pins

1. Go to Pinterest settings → Claim your website → enter unsnag.co
2. Pinterest will give you a meta tag to add to your site (tell Claude and he'll add it)
3. Rich Pins automatically pull blog post title, description, and favicon into the pin
