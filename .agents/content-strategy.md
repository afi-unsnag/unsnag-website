## Internal Linking Rules

Every new post should link to:
- 1-2 other blog posts in the same pillar (cross-linking)
- The launch page (at least once in the body or CTA)
- 1 post from a different pillar (for discovery)

---

## Publishing Cadence

- **First month:** 2 posts per week (build the library fast)
- **After that:** 1 post per week (sustainable)
- Prioritize searchable posts first to build organic traffic, then alternate with shareable posts for social

---

## Competitive Advantage

Your competitors (Calm, Headspace, BetterHelp) write clinical, generic wellness content. Your advantage:
- **Specificity** — the group chat, the unanswered text, the trivia night
- **Voice** — bold, funny, zero-bullshit vs. soft wellness
- **First-person authority** — Afi's story, not a faceless brand

The content that will rank isn't "5 tips for emotional wellness." It's "Why you assume everyone is mad at you" — because nobody else is writing that with this voice.
