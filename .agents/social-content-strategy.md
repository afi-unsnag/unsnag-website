# Unsnag Social Content Strategy

*Created: 2026-04-01*

---

## The System: One Blog Post → 5-8 Social Posts

| From Blog Post | Social Format | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| The headline | Bold text graphic (carousel slide 1 or single image) | Instagram, Pinterest |
| One key paragraph | Text screenshot or quote card | Instagram Stories, Pinterest |
| The "aha" line | Short-form video — you on camera saying just that line | Instagram Reels |
| The list/steps | Carousel (one point per slide) | Instagram, Pinterest |
| The personal story | Caption-heavy post with a photo of you | Instagram Feed |
| The question it answers | Poll or "this or that" | Instagram Stories |
| The closing line | Quote graphic | Pinterest, Instagram |

8 existing blog posts = 40-60 social posts ready to create.

---

## Content Pillars (Same as Blog)

**Feelings (mauve)** — the "oh my god, same" posts
- Gut-punch one-liners that make people tag friends
- "Thinking about a feeling is not the same as feeling it."

**Patterns (tomato)** — the "I feel seen" posts
- Name the pattern so specifically she screenshots it
- "You replay the text four times before sending it, then spend three hours wondering if it was weird."

**Identity (orange)** — the "permission" posts
- Give her permission to stop performing
- "You're allowed to want things just because you want them."

**Unsnag (blush)** — behind-the-scenes + product
- How you use it, why you built features, quick demos
- Keep to ~20% of content. 80% = value, not product.

---

## Instagram Format Mix

| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Carousels** | 2-3x/week | Education, lists, repurposed blog content. Highest save rate. |
| **Reels** (15-30 sec) | 2-3x/week | You on camera saying one thing. Reach + personality. |
| **Single image quotes** | 1-2x/week | Bold text on brand colors. Easy to share/save. |
| **Stories** | Daily | Behind the scenes, polls, "this or that," real-time moments. |
| **Feed captions** | With every post | Long, personal, written like a micro-blog. This is your voice. |

---

## Carousel Templates

**Structure 1: "You do this → here's why → here's what to do instead"**
- Slide 1: Bold hook ("You're not overthinking. You're stuck in a loop.")
- Slides 2-4: Explain why (from blog post body)
- Slide 5: What to do instead
- Slide 6: CTA ("Save this for next time it happens")

**Structure 2: "X signs you're [pattern]"**
- Slide 1: "5 signs you're people-pleasing (not just being nice)"
- Slides 2-6: One sign per slide
- Slide 7: "Sound familiar? Link in bio."

**Structure 3: "The difference between X and Y"**
- Slide 1: "Thinking about a feeling vs. actually feeling it"
- Slides 2-5: Side-by-side comparisons
- Slide 6: Bridge to Unsnag

---

## Reels Strategy

You, talking to the camera, saying ONE thing. 15-30 seconds. No transitions.

Lines from existing content:
- "A feeling fully felt lasts about 90 seconds. Nobody told me that."
- "I used to need everyone around me to be okay before I could be okay."
- "People-pleasing isn't a personality trait. It's a survival strategy."
- "You can't mute sadness and expect joy to stay loud."
- "I built an app because I was tired of spiraling until Thursday's therapy session."

## Pinterest Strategy

Pinterest is a search engine. It drives traffic to your blog.

**What to pin:**
- Every blog post title as a designed pin (1000x1500px)
- Quote graphics from each post
- Carousel slides as individual pins
- "How to" pins linking to blog posts

**Board ideas:**
- Overthinking & How to Stop
- People-Pleasing Recovery
- Feeling Your Feelings
- Emotional Wellness for Women
- Unsnag — The App

**Pin SEO:** Use keywords in titles/descriptions — same keywords as blog strategy.

## Automation System

**When you publish a blog post:**
1. Pull 3-5 standalone lines → **quote graphics**
2. Pull main argument into 5-6 slides → **carousel**
3. Record yourself saying the strongest line → **Reel**
4. Write a personal caption about why you wrote it → **feed post**
5. Create a tall pin with the title → **Pinterest**
6. Post a poll or "this or that" → **Stories**

**Tools:**
- **Canva** — brand templates for quote cards, carousels, pins (mauve, tomato, orange, blush on cream)
- **Later or Planoly** — schedule Instagram in advance
- **Tailwind** — schedule Pinterest, auto-loop best pins
- **Caption bank** — doc of best lines from blog posts

**Weekly time:** ~2-3 hours batched. Captions Monday, design Tuesday, schedule Wednesday.

## What NOT to Do

- Generic wellness quotes with sunset backgrounds
- Hashtags like #selfcare #wellness #healing
- Product screenshots as main content
- Filler posts just to be consistent
